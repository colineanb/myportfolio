Simplify to provide a complete experience without effort

As part of a school project, we had to imagine an application dedicated to book organized weekends "Airbnb weekends" created by Airbnb. The challenge of this application is to offer a complete experience including transport, accommodation and on-site experiences, while keeping Airbnb's identity.

How to offer an ultra-fast booking service for fully organized trips while creating an inspiring discovery experience for undecided travellers?

Stimulate the desire
and inspire the traveller

After studying the types of users and their needs, the user path was designed to satisfy the different profiles. Two possible paths, one when you have no idea of your destination, and one where the user already has an idea in mind. The goal : simplify the online booking process, and inspire the undecided traveller in an easy and assisted user experience.
